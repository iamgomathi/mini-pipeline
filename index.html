My Chennai — Quick Guide

Simple page with famous places, short phrases and speaking practice.

Places Food Speech

Marina Beach

One of the longest urban beaches. Good for morning walks and sunrise.

Use this phrase: "I love watching the sunrise at Marina Beach."

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

Historic temple in Mylapore with beautiful Dravidian architecture.

Try: "Kapaleeshwarar temple is beautiful and very old."

Santhome Church

A 400+ year old church built by the Portuguese over St. Thomas's tomb.

Say: "Santhome Church has fine white architecture and calm atmosphere."

Express Avenue / Phoenix Mall

Popular malls for shopping, movies and food.

Say: "I go to Phoenix Mall on weekends for movies and food."

Guindy National Park

A small green park inside the city with deer and birds.

Say: "Guindy is surprising — a green patch in the city to relax."

Mahabalipuram (near Chennai)

UNESCO site known for rock-cut temples and sculptures.

Say: "Mahabalipuram is a good day trip from Chennai for history lovers."

Famous Foods

Idli, dosa, filter coffee, biryani and seafood.

  • "I like filter coffee in the morning."
  • "Murugan Idli and Saravana Bhavan serve good traditional meals."

Short Speech — "My City: Chennai"

Practice this short speech slowly and clearly.

"Chennai is a coastal city in southern India. It has beautiful beaches like Marina and meaningful cultural places like Kapaleeshwarar Temple and Santhome Church. The food in Chennai is delicious, especially idli, dosa and filter coffee. I enjoy the mix of tradition and modern life in my city."

Tip: Read it twice — first for meaning, second slowly aloud for pronunciation practice.
